Lemon Chicken Orzo Soup

Listen. Everyone if going to love this soup, except for that one guy, Will, but who cares about him! All your guests will ask "Where can I get this recipe?" and "Does this have lemon in it?" Prepare for a warm hearty soup for the soul!

Ingredients

  • 1 pack orzo
  • 1 lb deboned chicken thighs
  • 2 lemons
  • 4 carrots
  • 1 onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 boxes chicken broth

Steps

  1. Cut chicken into bite sized pieces
  2. Cook in a small amount of oil before removing from the pot
  3. Add choped carrots onion and garlic to the warm pot. Cook until tender
  4. Add chicken broth and chicken back into the pot
  5. Add the desired amount of orzo and stir occasionally until Orzo is tender
  6. Finally add squeezed lemon juice into the soup, boiling for 2 more minutes
