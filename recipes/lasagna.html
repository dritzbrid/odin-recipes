Lasanga Recipe

The classic Lasagna dish isn't as hard as your Italian grandmother says it is. With just simple fresh ingredients, you'll be well on your way to the best meal ever!

Ingredients

  • 3 lb Ground beef
  • 1 Large can of tomato sauce
  • 8oz Ricotta Cheese
  • 1 pack lasanga noodles
  • Finly Grated Mozorella
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package
  3. Brown the beef
  4. Layer noodles, beef, rocotta cheese
  5. Finish with a layer of tomato sauce and finely grated mozzorella cheese
  6. Bake in a 350 degree oven for 45 min.
